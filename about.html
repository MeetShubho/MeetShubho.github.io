About Me

Why I am not your vanilla Project / Program / Product Manager

My journey in technology started as a developer, giving me a deep appreciation for how things work under the hood. Over time, I combined this technical foundation with business acumen gained through my MBA and experience as a Business Analyst. This unique blend allows me to connect technology with strategy and user needs effortlessly.

Along my professional journey, I have earned certifications like PMP, PMI-ACP, PSM I, PSPO I, SAFe Agilist, ICP-ACC, and AWS. Each of these has not only sharpened my knowledge but also shaped the way I approach leadership, agility, and delivering value.

I’ve led initiatives across Waterfall and Agile frameworks, and my work has spanned industries from Telecom and Infotainment to Banking, Finance, RPA, Infrastructure, and Product Development. Being domain-agnostic has made me adaptable, always ready to wear different hats and step into new challenges.

Today, I’m actively transitioning into Product Leadership. I believe in continuous reinvention, which is why I am pursuing the Executive Program in Product Management from IIT Guwahati. This complements my hands-on leadership experience and fuels my vision to build products that matter — ones that inspire confidence and solve real problems.

Beyond work, I’m an AI/ML enthusiast who loves exploring how technology transforms business and society. And at home, I’m a proud father, constantly inspired by the curiosity and wonder of my little one.
