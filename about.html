About Me

Hi, I’m Subhabrata Chattopadhyay — a builder of teams, a navigator of complex programs, and most importantly, a curious learner who loves turning challenges into opportunities.

My journey started in IT project management, where I spent over 15 years driving initiatives across global organizations. Along the way, I picked up not just certifications — PMP, PMI-ACP, PSM, PSPO, SAFe Agilist, AWS — but also the ability to bring structure, clarity, and empathy to any team I lead.

Over the years, I’ve worn many hats: Project Manager, Program Manager, Agile Coach, and now transitioning into Product Management & Leadership. What excites me most is building products that matter — solutions that don’t just solve problems, but inspire confidence in people who use them.

Outside of work, I’m an AI/ML enthusiast exploring the intersection of technology and business, and a proud father who learns daily from the curiosity of a little explorer at home.
