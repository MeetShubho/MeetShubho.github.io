About Me

Why I’m not your vanilla Project / Program / Product Manager

From Developer to Leader

I didn’t start my career with Gantt charts and status reports — I started as a developer. This foundation gives me a deep understanding of technology and empathy for engineering teams, enabling me to bridge the gap between strategy and execution.

Business Mindset

With an MBA and experience as a Business Analyst, I understand both business priorities and user needs. This helps me translate strategy into actionable roadmaps that deliver real impact, not just outputs.

Diverse Experience

I’ve worked across Telecom, Infotainment, RPA, Product Development, Infrastructure, Banking, and Finance. I’ve embraced multiple roles, worn different hats, and adapted quickly — proving I’m domain agnostic and thrive in dynamic environments.

Agile & Frameworks

From Waterfall to Agile, I understand how different frameworks work and how to apply them pragmatically. I focus on outcomes over outputs — empowering teams to deliver with clarity, adaptability, and collaboration.

Continuous Learning

Along my journey, I’ve earned certifications including PMP, PMI-ACP, PSM I, PSPO I, SAFe Agilist, ICP-ACC, and AWS. Each certification has not just validated my skills but also helped me grow as a professional and adapt to evolving challenges.

Transition to Product Leadership

Today, I’m reinventing myself once again — pursuing the Executive Program in Product Management from IIT Guwahati. My goal is to transition into Product Leadership roles, building solutions that don’t just solve problems but inspire confidence in the people who use them.
