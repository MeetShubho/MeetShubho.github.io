About Me

Why I’m not your vanilla Project / Program / Product Manager

My journey didn’t start with Gantt charts — it began with writing code. As a developer, I learned the mindset it takes to build technology that lasts, and that foundation has shaped everything I’ve done since.

I then pursued an MBA in Hannover and worked as a Business Analyst. This gave me a deep appreciation for both the business lens and the user’s perspective — helping me bridge strategy and execution seamlessly.

Over the years, I’ve led projects in both Waterfall and Agile frameworks, across domains like Telecom, Infotainment, RPA, Product Development, Infrastructure, Banking, and Finance. Each experience has made me more adaptable and domain-agnostic.

Reinvention is my constant. I’ve worn many hats — Project Manager, Program Manager, Agile Coach, Product Owner — and now I’m self-learning AI/ML to transition into Product Leadership.

I’m not just a manager. I’m a builder, learner, and connector who thrives at the intersection of technology, business, and people.
